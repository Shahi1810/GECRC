about us

Global Environmental Consultancy and Research Centre (GECRC), Lucknow is an ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015, OHSAS 18001:2007 certified & ISO/IEC 17025 (NABL) accredited Laboratory. GECRC was established in 2015 and is involved in the field of Environmental Monitoring & Analysis, Chemical and microbiological testing, Quality Management Services, Training Programs, R&D activities.

We also provide services like Operation & Maintenance of STP, ETP, CETP, WTP, Swimming Pool, Filtration System and supplying of waste water treatment systems & consumables. The product range offered by us includes Equipment & consumables for wastewater treatment plants, Sewage treatment pleants, Industrial effluent treatment plants, Swimming Pool Filtration System etc.

customer centric

One of the ways we create value is through the strength of the realtionships we build with our customers, as they play an important role in influencing the strategic direction of our business, so we have put in places systems and processes to ensure timely and quality services.
